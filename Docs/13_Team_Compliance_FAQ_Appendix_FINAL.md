## PROGRAM SUMMARY

Travel.SportsMedia.us provides a sports-specific request and authorization platform that routes charter mobility requests to properly certificated air carriers and/or properly licensed charter brokers for operational execution.

**Key Characteristics:**

- Technology platform, NOT an air carrier
- Supplemental and non-exclusive
- Voluntary for all teams
- Does NOT replace team charter operations

**Designed For:**

- Small-group, short-notice travel
- Executive mobility
- Player and family movement (non-team travel)
- Sponsor and VIP movement
- Urgent business needs

## SECTION 1: REGULATORY AND LEGAL QUESTIONS

### Q1: Are we buying air transportation from Travel.SportsMedia.us?

**No.** Teams are purchasing a **technology and coordination service**. Air transportation is provided only by certificated air carriers and, where applicable, licensed charter brokers and operators.

The Platform:

- Routes requests to Fulfillment Partners
- Manages approval workflows
- Tracks SLA performance
- Maintains compliance logs

The Platform does NOT:

- Operate aircraft
- Provide air transportation
- Contract for carriage

---

### Q2: Is Travel.SportsMedia.us an air carrier?

**No.** Travel.SportsMedia.us:

- Does NOT operate aircraft
- Does NOT provide air transportation services
- Does NOT hold an FAA Air Carrier Certificate
- Is NOT subject to Part 119 certification requirements

Travel.SportsMedia.us is a technology platform operator and licensed travel agency.

---

### Q3: Is Travel.SportsMedia.us acting as a charter broker?

**No.** Travel.SportsMedia.us does NOT:

- Select the air carrier for any flight
- Control aircraft operations or dispatch
- Hold out to the public as an air charter broker
- Execute charter contracts with operators
- Set or control air transportation prices

**Fulfillment Partners** (properly licensed charter brokers) source and contract the charter transportation.

---

### Q4: Who contracts for the flight?

The charter **contract for carriage** is between:

- The traveler or team entity (as applicable), AND
- The certificated air carrier and/or properly licensed broker

This contract is **separate from** any agreement with Travel.SportsMedia.us. The platform is not a party to the contract for carriage.

---

### Q5: What regulatory standards apply to the operator?

| Standard          | Requirement                                                |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| FAA Certification | Part 135 for on-demand charter                             |
| Safety Ratings    | ARGUS Gold/Platinum or Wyvern Registered/Wingman preferred |
| Insurance         | $25M-$50M aircraft liability                               |
| DOT Compliance    | Broker registration (Part 295) where applicable            |

The Fulfillment Partner is responsible for ensuring correct certification and compliance.

---

### Q6: What are the DOT disclosure requirements?

Under 14 CFR Part 295, charter brokers must disclose:

- Their capacity (broker vs. carrier)
- The existence or absence of liability insurance
- Upon request: relationship with carrier, total cost breakdown

These disclosures are the **Fulfillment Partner's responsibility**, not the Platform's.

## SECTION 2: PRICING AND PAYMENT QUESTIONS

### Q7: Who sets the flight price and provides quotes?

The **Fulfillment Partner and/or certificated air carrier** sets and issues:

- Flight quotes
- Pricing
- Terms and conditions

The Platform:

- Presents quotes received from Fulfillment Partners
- Does NOT set or control air transportation prices
- Does NOT negotiate pricing with operators

---

### Q8: Who collects payment for the charter flight?

| Payment Type           | Collected By                           |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Charter flight cost    | Fulfillment Partner and/or air carrier |
| Platform fees (if any) | Travel.SportsMedia.us                  |

Payments are collected under **separate agreements**:

- Charter contract (Fulfillment Partner)
- Platform services agreement (Travel.SportsMedia.us)

---

### Q9: What are the Platform fees?

Platform fees vary by service tier:

| Tier       | Structure                                  |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Standard   | Per-booking fee ($500-$750)                |
| Premium    | Reduced booking fees + annual subscription |
| Enterprise | Subscription with waived booking fees      |

**Platform fees are separate from charter costs** and are billed directly by Travel.SportsMedia.us.

---

### Q10: How are cancellations, delays, and refunds handled?

| Item                  | Governed By                                              |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Charter cancellations | Broker/carrier contract terms                            |
| Charter refunds       | Broker/carrier policies                                  |
| Flight delays         | Operator responsibility                                  |
| Platform fees         | Platform agreement (typically non-refundable at booking) |

The Platform does NOT override carrier cancellation policies.

## SECTION 3: SAFETY AND COMPLIANCE QUESTIONS

### Q11: How do we confirm the operator is compliant and insured?

The Fulfillment Partner is required to:

- Maintain current certifications
- Carry aviation liability insurance
- Verify operator credentials before each booking

**Available upon request:**

- Insurance certificates
- Operator certifications
- Safety rating reports
- FAA database verification

---

### Q12: What safety ratings are required?

| Organization | Minimum    | Preferred |
| ------------ | ---------- | --------- |
| ARGUS        | Gold       | Platinum  |
| Wyvern       | Registered | Wingman   |

**What these ratings mean:**

- Operator has passed safety audits
- Documented safety management systems
- No outstanding safety issues
- Regular re-certification required

---

### Q13: What liability does the team assume?

| Party               | Liability                                              |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Air Carrier         | Operational safety, regulatory compliance              |
| Fulfillment Partner | Broker obligations, operator selection                 |
| Team                | Accurate traveler info, lawful use, contract adherence |

The team is responsible for:

- Providing accurate passenger information
- Using services for lawful purposes
- Adhering to cancellation policies
- Payment obligations

---

### Q14: Does the Platform guarantee aircraft availability?

**No.** The Platform:

- Routes requests to Fulfillment Partners
- Monitors service levels
- Does NOT guarantee availability

Availability depends on:

- Aircraft supply
- Operator acceptance
- Market conditions
- Operational constraints (weather, crew, maintenance)

### Q15: What happens for last-minute or emergency requests?

| Action      | Response                                |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------- |

### Q16: Can we restrict who inside the team can request flights?

**Yes.** The Platform supports:

| Control                 | Implementation                           |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Role-Based Access       | Limit by department/role                 |
| Approval Workflows      | Require manager/finance approval         |
| Spending Limits         | Auto-approve below thresholds            |
| Department Restrictions | Executive, player services, partnerships |

---

### Q17: What use cases does the program cover?

| Covered          | Examples                                   |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Executive Travel | GM, President, ownership, board            |
| Player Movement  | Trades, transactions, physicals (non-team) |
| Family Travel    | Approved player/executive family           |
| Sponsor/VIP      | Partner hospitality, events                |

| NOT Covered         | Reason                        |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Team Charter        | Separate program              |
| CBA-Governed Travel | League/union agreements       |
| Medical Evacuation  | Requires specialized services |

---

### Q18: Does using the Platform conflict with existing providers?

**No.** The program is:

- **Supplemental** - additional option, not replacement
- **Non-exclusive** - teams may use other providers
- **Voluntary** - no obligation to use

Designed for edge cases and short-notice needs not handled by primary travel arrangements.

---

### Q19: Does the Platform facilitate international charters?

**Yes, with limitations:**

| Aspect                | Status                             |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Request Routing       | Supported                          |
| Regulatory Compliance | Fulfillment Partner responsibility |
| Permits/Customs       | Operator/broker managed            |
| Availability          | Varies by destination              |

International regulatory requirements are complex and vary by country.
