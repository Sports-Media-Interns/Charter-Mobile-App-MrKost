## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Authorized User"** means any individual designated by Client to access and use the Platform, including travel managers, executives, and administrative staff.

**1.2 "Charter Request"** means a request for charter aviation services submitted through the Platform.

**1.3 "Charter Broker"** means Chief Executive Air or other qualified charter brokerage partner(s) engaged by Sports Media.

**1.4 "Completed Flight"** means a charter flight that has been executed with passengers transported from origin to destination.

**1.5 "Operator"** means the FAA Part 135 certificated air carrier that operates the aircraft.

**1.6 "Platform"** means the Sports Media Charter mobile application, web portal, and associated services.

**1.7 "Quote"** means a price proposal for charter services including aircraft options, pricing, and terms.

**1.8 "Service Tier"** means the level of platform access and support selected by Client.

## ARTICLE 2: IMPORTANT DISCLOSURES

### 2.1 Non-Air Carrier Status

**SPORTS MEDIA IS A LICENSED TRAVEL AGENCY AND IS NOT AN AIR CARRIER.**

- Sports Media does not operate aircraft
- Sports Media does not have operational control of any flights
- All flights are operated by FAA Part 135 certificated air carriers
- The Charter Broker arranges flights with qualified Operators
- The Operator maintains full operational control and responsibility for flight safety

### 2.2 Role of Sports Media

Sports Media's role is limited to:

- Operating the Sports Media Charter platform technology
- Facilitating charter requests between Client and Charter Broker
- Providing platform support and account management
- Processing payments on behalf of Charter Broker and Operators

### 2.3 Role of Charter Broker

The Charter Broker (Chief Executive Air):

- Sources aircraft and provides quotes
- Arranges bookings with certificated Operators
- Provides 24/7 operational support
- Delivers compliance documentation
- Is subject to DOT regulations under 14 CFR Part 295

### 2.4 Role of Operators

Aircraft Operators:

- Hold FAA Part 135 Air Carrier Certificates
- Maintain operational control of all flights
- Carry required aircraft liability insurance
- Are responsible for flight safety and crew qualifications

This Agreement automatically renews for successive one (1) year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days before expiration.

### 12.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:

- Materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Ceases business operations

### 12.4 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate for convenience with sixty (60) days written notice.

### 12.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

- Platform access terminates immediately
- Client shall pay all outstanding fees

- **Termination by Sports Media for Cause:** No refund
