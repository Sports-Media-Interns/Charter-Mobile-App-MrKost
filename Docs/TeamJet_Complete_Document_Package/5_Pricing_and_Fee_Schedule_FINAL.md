## SECTION 1: FIXED FEES PER COMPLETED FLIGHT

### 1.1 Fee Structure

Broker shall pay Sports Media the following fixed fees for each Completed Flight:

| Aircraft Category     | Representative Aircraft                               | Passenger Capacity | Fixed Fee  |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------ | ---------- |
| **Turboprop**         | King Air 350, Pilatus PC-12, Beechcraft 1900          | 6-19               | **$1,250** |
| **Light Jet**         | Citation CJ3/CJ4, Phenom 300, Learjet 45              | 6-8                | **$1,500** |
| **Midsize Jet**       | Citation XLS+, Learjet 60, Hawker 800XP               | 7-9                | **$1,750** |
| **Super-Midsize Jet** | Citation Sovereign, Challenger 350, Citation Latitude | 8-12               | **$2,250** |
| **Heavy Jet**         | Gulfstream G450/G550, Challenger 650, Falcon 900      | 10-16              | **$3,000** |
| **Ultra-Long Range**  | Gulfstream G650, Global 6000/7500, Falcon 8X          | 12-19              | **$3,500** |
| **VIP Airliner**      | Boeing BBJ, Airbus ACJ, Embraer Lineage               | 19+                | **$5,000** |

### 1.2 Category Determination

- Aircraft category determined by actual aircraft flown
- If aircraft falls between categories, higher category applies
- Multi-leg trips: fee applies to each leg flown
- Repositioning legs (empty): no fee charged

### 1.3 Completed Flight Definition

A "Completed Flight" means:

- Passengers boarded at origin
- Aircraft departed
- Aircraft arrived at destination
- Passengers deplaned

**Excluded from fees:**

- Cancelled flights (regardless of timing)
- Diverted flights that do not reach intended destination
- Flights terminated for safety reasons before departure

**All platform service fees are 100% retained by Sports Media** with no revenue sharing obligation to Broker.

## SECTION 4: QUARTERLY PERFORMANCE BONUSES

### 4.1 Volume Bonus Tiers

| Quarterly Volume       | Bonus Amount | Cumulative |
| ---------------------- | ------------ | ---------- |
| 25+ Completed Flights  | $10,000      | $10,000    |
| 50+ Completed Flights  | $25,000      | $35,000    |
| 100+ Completed Flights | $50,000      | $85,000    |

### 4.2 Bonus Calculation

- Measured per calendar quarter (Jan-Mar, Apr-Jun, Jul-Sep, Oct-Dec)
- All aircraft categories count equally toward volume
- Multi-leg trips: each leg counts as separate flight
- Bonuses are cumulative (50 flights = $35,000 total)

### 4.3 Bonus Payment

- Calculated within 15 days of quarter end
- Paid via wire transfer within 30 days of quarter end
- Broker provides flight log reconciliation with payment

## SECTION 5: PAYMENT TERMS

### 5.1 Broker Payments to Sports Media

| Payment Type      | Timing                               | Method   |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------- |
| Fixed Fees        | Monthly, within 15 days of month-end | Wire/ACH |
| Quarterly Bonuses | Within 30 days of quarter-end        | Wire/ACH |

### 5.2 Required Documentation

Monthly payment must include:

- Detailed booking statement (Excel format)
- Flight manifest summary
- Aircraft category verification
- Retainer recovery tracking (if applicable)

### 5.3 Late Payment Penalties

| Days Late  | Penalty                                         |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| 1-15 days  | 1.5% of amount due                              |
| 16-30 days | 3.0% of amount due                              |
| 31+ days   | 5.0% of amount due + suspension of new bookings |

### 5.4 Disputed Amounts

- Broker must notify Sports Media of disputes within 10 days
- Undisputed amounts due regardless of disputes
- Disputed amounts resolved within 30 days
- Interest accrues on amounts determined owed

| Factor       | Impact                                     |

| Catering     | Premium catering adds 5-15%                |
| Wi-Fi/Tech   | Connectivity requirements may add cost     |

## SECTION 9: ADJUSTMENTS AND AMENDMENTS

### 9.1 Annual Review

- Fee schedule reviewed annually on Agreement anniversary
- Adjustments require mutual written agreement
- 60 days notice for any proposed changes

### 9.2 Inflation Adjustment

- Fixed fees may be adjusted annually for CPI-U
- Maximum annual increase: 5%
- Adjustment calculated on Agreement anniversary

### 9.3 New Categories

- New aircraft categories may be added by mutual agreement
- Pricing for new categories determined collaboratively

## SECTION 10: AUDIT RIGHTS

### 10.1 Sports Media Audit Rights

Sports Media may audit Broker records to verify:

- Accurate aircraft category reporting
- Completed flight counts
- Proper fee calculation

### 10.2 Audit Process

- 30 days written notice required
- Audit during normal business hours
- Broker provides reasonable access to records
- Audit limited to once per calendar year

### 10.3 Discrepancies

- Discrepancies resolved within 30 days
- Underpayments paid with 1.5% monthly interest
- Overpayments credited to next statement
- Material discrepancies (>5%) may trigger additional audit rights
