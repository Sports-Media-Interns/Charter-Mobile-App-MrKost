## ARTICLE 1: PROGRAM OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Program Description

The Sports Media Charter Charter Procurement Program ("Program") provides League member teams with:

- Access to a secure, purpose-built charter procurement platform
- Pre-vetted charter brokerage services through qualified partners
- Standardized safety and compliance documentation
- Consistent service levels and pricing transparency
- Centralized reporting and audit capabilities

### 1.2 Program Scope

The Program covers non-team charter aviation services including:

| Category         | Examples                                   |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Executive Travel | Team executives, ownership, board members  |
| Front Office     | General managers, scouts, operations staff |
| Player Movement  | Trades, transactions, personal logistics   |
| Family Travel    | Approved player and staff family members   |
| Sponsor/VIP      | Partner hospitality, activation events     |
| Draft/Events     | Combines, drafts, league meetings          |
| Emergency        | Medical, security, crisis situations       |

### 1.3 Exclusions

This Program does NOT cover:

- Full team charter operations (roster travel)
- League-mandated team travel
- Any travel governed by collective bargaining agreements
- Air ambulance or medical evacuation services

### 1.4 Voluntary Participation

**Team participation in this Program is entirely voluntary.**

- League is not mandating use of the Program
- Teams may continue using existing charter arrangements
- No team will be penalized for non-participation
- Teams may opt in or out at any time

## ARTICLE 2: REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

### 2.1 Sports Media Status

**Sports Media is a licensed travel agency and NOT an air carrier.**

- Sports Media does not operate aircraft
- Sports Media does not have operational control of flights
- Sports Media facilitates connections between teams and charter brokers
- All flights operated by FAA Part 135 certificated air carriers

### 2.2 Broker Partner Status

Sports Media's charter broker partner (Chief Executive Air):

- Operates under DOT regulations (14 CFR Part 295)
- Is NOT an air carrier
- Arranges flights with certificated operators only
- Maintains required broker disclosures and insurance

### 2.3 Operator Requirements

All aircraft operators in the Program must:

| Requirement               | Standard                                           |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| FAA Certificate           | Valid Part 135 Air Carrier Certificate             |
| Operations Specifications | Current and in good standing                       |
| Safety Audit              | ARGUS Gold/Platinum or Wyvern Registered/Wingman   |
| Insurance                 | Minimum $25M-$50M aircraft liability               |
| FAA Status                | No current enforcement actions or emergency orders |
| Drug Testing              | FAA-compliant drug and alcohol testing program     |

### 2.4 Compliance with League Policies

Sports Media agrees to:

- Comply with all applicable League vendor policies
- Maintain appropriate insurance coverage
- Submit to League compliance audits upon request
- Report any incidents or safety concerns promptly
- Protect League and team confidential information

## ARTICLE 5: PROGRAM STANDARDS

### 5.1 Safety Standards

**Operator Vetting:**

- All operators verified against FAA database before each booking
- Safety ratings confirmed (ARGUS/Wyvern)
- Insurance certificates obtained
- No flights on operators with pending enforcement actions

**Incident Reporting:**

- Any safety incident reported to League within 24 hours
- Written incident report within 72 hours
- Corrective action plans developed as needed
- Quarterly safety summary provided to League

### 5.2 Service Level Standards

| Metric                         | Standard     |
| ------------------------------ | ------------ |
| Urgent Quote Response (<6 hrs) | 30 minutes   |
| Standard Quote Response        | 2 hours      |
| Booking Confirmation           | 1 hour       |
| Insurance Certificate Delivery | 4 hours      |
| Platform Availability          | 99.5% uptime |
| Support Response (Enterprise)  | 15 minutes   |

### 5.3 Data Protection Standards

Sports Media shall:

- Encrypt all data in transit and at rest
- Implement role-based access controls
- Maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance (or roadmap)
- Conduct annual penetration testing
- Not sell or share team/player data with third parties
- Comply with applicable privacy laws (CCPA, etc.)

### 5.4 Financial Standards

- Transparent, all-inclusive pricing
- No hidden fees or surcharges
- Clear cancellation policies
- PCI DSS compliant payment processing
- Segregated client funds (where applicable)
- Monthly financial reporting to teams

## ARTICLE 15: COMPLIANCE FAQ

### For Team Operations Staff

**Q: Is this program mandatory?**
A: No. Team participation is entirely voluntary.

**Q: Does this affect our team charter (roster travel)?**
A: No. This program covers executive and auxiliary travel only, not team charter operations.

**Q: Can we continue using our existing charter broker?**
A: Yes. Teams may maintain existing relationships for travel outside this Program.

**Q: What safety standards are required?**
A: All operators must be FAA Part 135 certificated with ARGUS Gold/Platinum or Wyvern Registered/Wingman ratings.

**Q: Who has operational control of flights?**
A: The aircraft operator (Part 135 certificate holder) maintains full operational control. Sports Media and the broker do not operate aircraft.

### For Team Compliance/Legal

**Q: What insurance coverage is in place?**
A: Multiple layers: (1) Operator aircraft liability ($25-50M), (2) Broker E&O ($2M), (3) Sports Media professional liability ($2M).

**Q: Is data protected?**
A: Yes. Sports Media maintains SOC 2 compliance, encrypts all data, and complies with CCPA and applicable privacy laws.

**Q: What happens if there's a safety incident?**
A: Sports Media reports to League within 24 hours, provides written report within 72 hours, and develops corrective action plans.

**Q: Can the League audit the program?**
A: Yes. League has audit rights with reasonable notice.

### For Team Finance

**Q: How is pricing determined?**
A: Charter pricing is market-based, provided by the broker. Platform fees are fixed and transparent.

**Q: What are the payment terms?**
A: Charter deposits due at booking, balance 48 hours before departure. Platform fees are Net 30.

**Q: Are there volume discounts?**
A: Enterprise tier subscribers receive preferred pricing and waived platform fees.

**Q: What are the cancellation policies?**
A: Varies by operator, clearly disclosed in each quote. Typically ranges from 0% (72+ hours) to 100% (no-show).
