## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Aircraft Operator"** means any FAA Part 135 certificated air carrier that operates aircraft under direct operational control.

**1.2 "ARGUS"** means Aviation Research Group United States, an independent aviation safety auditing organization.

**1.3 "Charter Request"** means any request for air charter services submitted through the Sports Media Charter platform or any Sports Media channel.

**1.4 "Client"** means any team, organization, sponsor, executive, or individual accessing charter services through Sports Media.

**1.5 "Completed Flight"** means a flight that has been executed with passengers transported from origin to destination (wheels up to wheels down), excluding cancellations, diversions resulting in non-completion, or flights terminated due to safety concerns.

**1.6 "DOT"** means the United States Department of Transportation.

**1.7 "FAA"** means the Federal Aviation Administration.

**1.8 "Gross Charter Price"** means the total amount charged to the Client for a charter flight, inclusive of all fees, taxes, and surcharges.

**1.9 "Net Margin"** means Gross Charter Price minus Operator Cost minus Pass-Through Expenses minus applicable taxes.

**1.10 "Operator Cost"** means the amount paid by Broker to the Aircraft Operator for flight services.

**1.11 "Part 135"** means 14 CFR Part 135, the FAA regulations governing on-demand air charter operations.

**1.12 "Part 295"** means 14 CFR Part 295, the DOT regulations governing air charter brokers.

**1.13 "Pass-Through Expenses"** means direct costs including but not limited to landing fees, handling fees, overnight fees, catering, ground transportation, international handling, customs, permits, and de-icing.

**1.14 "Platform"** means the Sports Media Charter mobile application and web portal operated by Sports Media.

**1.15 "SLA"** means Service Level Agreement as defined in Schedule A.

**1.16 "Urgent Request"** means any Charter Request with requested departure within six (6) hours of submission.

**1.17 "Wyvern"** means Wyvern Ltd., an independent aviation safety auditing organization.

## ARTICLE 4: REGULATORY DISCLOSURES AND COMPLIANCE

**4.1 Sports Media Status.** Sports Media is:

- A licensed travel agency operating under applicable state regulations
- NOT an air carrier under 14 CFR Part 119
- NOT in operational control of any aircraft
- Acting as either an agent of the charterer or as a platform facilitating charter procurement

**4.2 Broker Status.** Chief Executive Air operates as an air charter broker subject to 14 CFR Part 295 and shall:

- Maintain compliance with all DOT disclosure requirements
- Clearly disclose its status as a broker (not an air carrier) in all communications
- Arrange flights only with properly certificated Aircraft Operators
- Provide all required disclosures under Part 295.24, including:
  - The capacity in which Broker is acting
  - The existence or absence of liability insurance held by Broker
  - Upon request: relationship with Aircraft Operator, total cost breakdown

**4.3 Operator Requirements.** All Aircraft Operators arranged by Broker must:

- Hold a valid FAA Part 135 Air Carrier Certificate
- Maintain current Operations Specifications (OpSpecs)
- Carry aircraft liability insurance of not less than:
  - $50,000,000 combined single limit for aircraft with 10+ passenger seats
  - $25,000,000 combined single limit for aircraft with fewer than 10 passenger seats
- Maintain a current safety rating from ARGUS (Gold or Platinum) or Wyvern (Registered or Wingman)
- Be in good standing with the FAA with no current enforcement actions

**4.4 Prohibited Arrangements.** Broker shall NOT:

- Arrange "grey charter" or Part 91 flights marketed as charter
- Use operators with revoked, suspended, or expired certificates
- Arrange flights with operators under FAA emergency orders
- Sell individual seats on Part 135 aircraft (prohibited under Part 295)
- Misrepresent pricing, aircraft type, or operator credentials

## ARTICLE 9: EXCLUSIVITY AND PERFORMANCE PROTECTION

**9.1 Exclusive Launch Partnership.** Chief Executive Air shall be the exclusive charter brokerage partner for all Charter Requests at launch and throughout the term, subject to the performance protections below.

**9.2 Performance Protection Triggers.** Sports Media may route Charter Requests to a secondary broker without terminating this Agreement if any of the following occur:

(a) **Urgent SLA Failures:** Three (3) or more Urgent Request SLA misses within any rolling thirty (30) day period

(b) **Cumulative SLA Failures:** Five (5) or more total SLA misses (any category) within any rolling sixty (60) day period

(c) **Availability Failures:** Failure to provide any viable quote option for three (3) or more requests within thirty (30) days where market availability reasonably exists

(d) **Service Failures:** Two (2) or more confirmed client complaints regarding Broker service quality within any sixty (60) day period, verified through documented feedback

(e) **Compliance Failures:** Any instance of arranging flights with non-compliant operators, failure to deliver required documentation, or material breach of Part 295 requirements

**9.3 Cure Process.**

(a) Sports Media shall provide written notice specifying the performance issue(s)

(b) Broker shall have fifteen (15) business days to cure and demonstrate restored performance

(c) During the cure period, Sports Media may route affected request categories to secondary providers

(d) Upon successful cure, exclusive routing shall resume

**9.4 Continued Relationship.** Triggering the secondary broker provision does NOT:

- Terminate this Agreement
- Release either party from ongoing obligations
- Affect compensation for bookings fulfilled by Broker
- Eliminate Broker's opportunity to regain exclusive status

**9.5 Return to Exclusivity.** Following any secondary broker activation, Broker may return to exclusive status upon:

- Thirty (30) consecutive days of full SLA compliance
- Written certification of corrective actions taken
- Sports Media approval (not to be unreasonably withheld)

See **Schedule G** for detailed performance metrics and measurement formulas.

- Share Client information with competing platforms or brokers

**14.2 Duration.** Non-circumvention obligations continue for twenty-four (24) months following:

- Expiration or termination of this Agreement, OR

**14.3 Exceptions.** This provision does not apply to:
